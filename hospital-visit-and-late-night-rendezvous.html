Hospital visit and late-night rendezvous

From onyo

12-30-24

I was horribly sick and waiting in a hospital's urgent care reception area. Once it was finally my turn, I was taken to a room where I spoke with a reception nurse. She asked me what I was visiting for, to which I said bad abdominal pain and nausea. Upon hearing this, she seemed to understand and mentioned a really bad virus going around. She then asked me if I was having pain "back there" I said yes. She wrote that down too and left the room, saying she'd be back with the actual doctor.

Minutes later, the two returned and asked more about the pain "back there". I described it and upon finishing the doc said she was gonna prescribe an oil with painkillers/anesthetic in it that I am to apply with a rod. Seeing that the idea put me off, she assured me that this was the only way to stop the bowel/abdomen pain due to how this virus works. She finished off by saying that I am more than free to keep suffering if I choose not to. She made the prescription for the oil and an anti nausea medication and sent me on my way.

I had left the extremely confusing hospital campus (there were only one-way roads leading into the campus and towards the main building) and headed towards the pharmacy. I parked a bit away from the pharmacy and was going to walk the rest of the short way. Almost as soon as I exited my car, I ran into zako and monax. They said they were on their way to a room that they rented out for the night and invited me to join. I said I could join for a bit and followed them.

Once we got to the room, zako and monax started to take their clothes off and I kinda started to panic. I asked what exactly we were doing, to which they asked me "isn't it obvious?" I just stood there stunned as they took off more and moved towards each other, asking me to join in already. Then I woke up.
