From onyo

07-25-2025

I was outside next to my car and talking with my sister. The sun was getting low, and the sky was overcast so it was getting dark fast. Suddenly, a black minivan pulls in to the driveway, and a couple gets out. They both looked Mexican and had black hair. The man was wearing sunglasses, so I could not see his eyes. The woman was wearing a large brimmed hat, and must have had her hair tied up within it. Both had dark hair and tanned skin. Neither of them look familiar.

The man, in Spanish, says he's here to deliver a TV. I ask him who he is, and he doesn't answer the question. Instead he steps back, opens the rear door, and pulls out a TV box that is either empty, or that he can easily move with one hand. He sets the box down and just looks at me. I ask again who he is, and he nods to the woman. The woman then rapidly walks to our front door, opens it, and enters the house. The man says and does nothing, as if he's waiting for something.
